About Us

The "Women and Gender Minorities in Tech" community,
born out of the Microsoft Learn Student Ambassadors
program, stands as a testament to the commitment
towards fostering inclusivity and diversity in the tech
industry. This community serves as a supportive platform
where individuals from various backgrounds come together
to share experiences, insights, and resources.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a welcoming and supportive environment
where women and gender minorities can thrive in the tech
industry. We strive to empower individuals through education,
mentorship, and community-building initiatives.
